Mission

Restore mechanical reliability for farm operations on challenging terrain:

  • 10-mile mountain road, twice daily
  • Steep grades and dirt roads
  • All-weather capability required
  • Hot summers, snowy winters

Philosophy: All effort and money goes to mechanical reliability. Embrace the "apocalyptic" aesthetic.
